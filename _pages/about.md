My name is pronounced like "Tungho Chang." [How to pronounce Zhang?](https://pronouncenames.com/pronounce/Zhang)


Welcome to my homepage! I am a final-year undergraduate student at [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), majoring in [Electronic Engineering](https://www.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/).

I am passionate about developing efficient and theoretically grounded **machine learning** algorithms, particularly in reinforcement learning (RL) and imitation learning (IL). I also explore related fields like statistical and online learning, stochastic and distributed optimization, and applying RL/IL in **robot learning** tasks such as locomotion and manipulation.

During my studies, I worked with Prof. [Longbo Huang](https://people.iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/~huang/) at [IIIS, Tsinghua University](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/en) on risk-sensitive RL and with Prof. [Yuejie Chi](https://users.ece.cmu.edu/~yuejiec/) and Dr. [Sudeep Salgia](https://sudeepsalgia.github.io/) at [ECE, Carnegie Mellon University](https://www.ece.cmu.edu/) on distributed partially observable RL. I enjoyed a rewarding summer at the embodied AI startup [Noetix Robotics](https://noetixrobotics.com/), training humanoid robots to run and spin like humans. I am currently working on my graduation thesis on diffusion policies under the supervision of Prof. [Yu Wang](https://nicsefc.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/people/YuWang).
