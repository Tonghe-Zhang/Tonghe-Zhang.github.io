Welcome to my homepage! I am a final-year undergraduate student at [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), majoring in [Electronic Engineering](https://www.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/).

I am passionate about developing empirically efficient and theoretically grounded **machine learning** algorithms, especially in reinforcement learning (RL) and imitation learning (IL). I also have a keen interest in related areas such as statistical and online learning, stochastic and distributed optimization, and applying RL/IL in **robot learning** tasks like locomotion and manipulation.

During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. [Longbo Huang](https://people.iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/~huang/) at [IIIS, Tsinghua University](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/en) for my research on risk-sensitive RL. Prof. [Yuejie Chi](https://users.ece.cmu.edu/~yuejiec/) and Dr. [Sudeep Salgia](https://sudeepsalgia.github.io/) at [ECE, Carnegie Mellon University](https://www.ece.cmu.edu/) provided substantial support in my project on distributed and partially observable RL. I also spent a wonderful summer at [Noetix Robotics](https://noetixrobotics.com/), working with colleagues to train humanoid robots to run, spin, and sidestep like humans. I am honored to be supervised by Professor [Yu Wang](https://nicsefc.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/people/YuWang) for my ongoing graduation thesis on diffusion policies.

**I am seeking a PhD position for Fall 2025.**

My name is pronounced roughly as 'Tungho Chang'. Click [here](https://pronouncenames.com/pronounce/Zhang) for a guide on how to correctly pronounce my family name 'Zhang'.
