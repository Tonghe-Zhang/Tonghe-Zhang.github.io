My name is pronounced like "Tungho Chang." [How to pronounce Zhang?](https://pronouncenames.com/pronounce/Zhang)

Welcome to my homepage! I am a final-year undergraduate student at [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), majoring in [Electronic Engineering](https://www.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/).

I am passionate about developing efficient and theoretically grounded **machine learning** algorithms, especially in reinforcement learning (RL) and imitation learning (IL), that can be applied to practical **robot learning** tasks, such as locomotion and manipulation.

Currently, I am working on fine-tuning flow-matching or diffusion policies using reinforcement learning.

I have industrial experience in training humanoid bipedal robots for locomotion. Additionally, I have led research projects in academia focusing on statistical reinforcement learning and distributed optimization, addressing real-world challenges such as risk and incomplete information in decision-making.

I am open to collaborating with professionals from diverse fields. Please feel free to contact me via email at zhang-th21[at]mails[dot]tsinghua[dot]edu[dot]cn or tonghezhang01[at]gmail[dot]com.
